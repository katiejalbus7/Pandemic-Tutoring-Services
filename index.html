Who Are We?

As the 2020-21 school year approaches, the COVID-19 pandemic continues to force many school districts to designate learning to be completed virtually. Pandemic Tutoring Services is a volunteer organization created to supply additional 1 on 1 and small group services to students for free as they adapt to learning online. This organization was formed by and consists of Michigan State students with a mission to support learning within the Lansing School District for the duration of the coronavirus pandemic. This has been and will continue to be a difficult year; however, when we band together, we can overcome the challenges which arise from this virus.

Tutoring Services

We are offering FREE tutoring services for students grade 4-12 in the Lansing School District via video chat in Math and English. If there is another subject you or your student needs extra support in, please feel free to email Pandemic Tutoring Services at pandemictutoringsupport@gmail.com and apply through the forms below. We are here to support you and will do our best in finding tutors to guide you through this new challenge of virtual learning.
